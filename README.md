## The problem it solves

1. 'FundeX Genesis' simplifies the process of making charitable donations by leveraging blockchain technology. <br>
2. Users can easily browse through various charitable causes, contribute to existing fundraising campaigns, or create their own campaigns. <br>
3. By utilizing cryptocurrency, the platform ensures fast, transparency and security in donation transactions, providing donors with confidence that their contributions are making a meaningful impact. <br>
4. We have also planned a proper revenue model with pricing slabs for future scalability and business perspective <br>
5. Future improvements include providing customised reccomendations to Donators and AI based seggregation.
